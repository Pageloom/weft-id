# Password

Manage your password from **Account > Password**.

## Changing your password

Enter your current password, then choose a new one. As you type, a strength meter shows how strong your password is, along with an estimated crack time and suggestions for improvement. Your new password must meet your organization's minimum length and strength requirements.

We recommend using a password manager to generate and store a strong, unique password.

## Forgot your password?

If you forget your password, click **Forgot password?** on the sign-in page (shown after entering your email). Enter your email address and you will receive a reset link. The link expires after 30 minutes.

Click the link to set a new password. After resetting, you are returned to the sign-in page to sign in with your new password.

!!! tip
    If you don't receive the reset email, check your spam folder. If you still don't see it, contact your administrator.

## Password reset required

Your administrator (or an automatic security check) may require you to change your password. When this happens, you see a "Password Reset Required" page after entering your current password during sign-in. Choose a new password to continue.

## Password requirements

Your organization's administrator sets the password policy. All passwords must:

* Meet a minimum length (shown on the password form)
* Reach a minimum strength score based on pattern analysis
* Not appear in known data breach databases

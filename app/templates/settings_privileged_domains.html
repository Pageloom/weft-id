Privileged Domains

Privileged domains are email domains that your organization controls or that you have chosen to trust (e.g., @company.com, @company.org). Users created with email addresses from these domains are automatically verified. Additionally, domains can be bound to an Identity Provider to automatically assign users to that IdP.

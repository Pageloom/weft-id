# Profile

Edit your personal information and preferences from the profile page at **Account > Profile**.

## Name

Update your first and last name. This may be disabled by your administrator.

## Theme

Choose between light mode, dark mode, or system (follows your device's preference).

## Regional settings

Your timezone and locale are detected automatically from your browser. Click **Update** to save the detected values. These settings control how dates and times are displayed.

## Email addresses

View your email addresses at **Account > Emails**. Your primary email is used for sign-in and SAML assertions. Email changes (adding, removing, or promoting) are managed by your administrator.

## Account information

The profile page also displays read-only account details: your role, user ID, tenant ID, and last sign-in time.
